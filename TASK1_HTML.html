LuminousKey is a Jordan-based technology firm specializing in digital transformation solutions, particularly within the healthcare sector across the Middle East. Established in 2011, the company offers a comprehensive range of services, including project management, system implementation, data migration, and custom software development.

Their expertise encompasses various domains such as cybersecurity, artificial intelligence in radiology, electronic medical records (EMR), business intelligence, and automation. Additionally, LuminousKey provides digital marketing services, covering graphic design, SEO, content creation, and multimedia production.

With a presence in Jordan, the UAE, and Saudi Arabia, LuminousKey collaborates with international partners to deliver tailored solutions that align with regional needs and industry standards.

Project Steps

  1. Business Analysis: Understand client needs and define the project scope clearly.
  2. Planning: Create a detailed project plan, including the timeline and task distribution.
  3. Design and Development: Design the system or digital solution based on requirements, then begin programming and development.
  4. Testing: Thoroughly test the system to ensure it's bug-free and performs as expected.
  5. Deployment: Once quality is confirmed, launch the solution in the client's environment.
  6. Training: Train users or staff on how to effectively use the system.
  7. Post-Delivery Support: Provide ongoing support for maintenance, updates, or troubleshooting.
